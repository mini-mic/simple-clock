# Privacy Policy for Simple Clock

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the Simple Clock application.

### 1. Data Collection
Simple Clock is a minimalist utility app. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** - No location tracking.
- No contact list access.
- No analytics or third-party tracking.

### 2. Device Permissions
The app may require the "Wake Lock" permission purely to keep the screen on while the clock is active. This data never leaves your device.

### 3. Contact
If you have questions, contact support@mehmetech.com
